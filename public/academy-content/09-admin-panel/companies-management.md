# Companies Management

The **Companies** tab allows Super Admins to monitor and manage all companies registered on the Traxxia platform.

Each company represents an organization using the system.

## Viewing Companies

The Companies dashboard displays a table containing important company information:

- Company Name
- Administrator
- Industry
- Company Size
- Status
- Total Users
- Created Date

![Companies Management](/academy-screenshots/admin-panel/companies.png)
 

## Company Status

Each company has a **status indicator** showing its current operational state.

Examples include:

- **Active** – The company is currently using the platform.
- **Inactive** – The company account is disabled or inactive.
 

## Search and Filtering

Administrators can quickly locate companies using the **search bar**.

This allows filtering companies by:

- Company name
- Administrator name
- Email address
