# Businesses Management

The **Businesses** tab displays all business workspaces created by companies on the platform.

Each business represents a strategic workspace where users conduct planning, analysis, and project management.

---

## Business Overview Table

The Businesses table displays:

- Business Name
- Owner
- Collaborators
- Created Date

![Businesses Overview](/academy-screenshots/admin-panel/businesses.png)

---

## Monitoring Business Activity

Super Admins can use this page to:

- Track how many businesses exist
- Identify business owners
- Monitor collaboration activity
- Review workspace creation trends

---

## Search Businesses

The search feature allows administrators to locate specific businesses by:

- Business name
- Owner name
- Owner email
