# Multiple Document Ingestion & AI Answers Extraction

To streamline the setup of your business profile, Traxxia supports **Multiple Document Upload** directly within the **Answers / Brief** interface of the **Analysis** tab. This powerful feature allows you to upload existing strategic plans, brochures, pitch decks, and financial statements, then let the AI automatically extract answers to the strategic questionnaire.
 

## Key Features

*   **Broad File Format Support**: Upload strategic documents and spreadsheets in one go. Accepted formats include:
    *   **Strategic Files**: PDF, DOCX (Word), or DOC.
    *   **Financial & Data Files**: Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV.
*   **5-File Upload Limit**: Upload up to **5 files** per business profile simultaneously to build a comprehensive contextual base.
*   **Sequential Upload Engine**: Features a real-time sequential validation and upload engine. Each file displays a progress bar (0-100%) showing validation and database upload status.
*   **Spreadsheet Parsing**: Financial spreadsheets are automatically routed to the financial indicators calculator, unlocking profitability, growth, liquidity, and leverage analysis.
 

## Strategic AI Extraction & Citations

Once your strategic files are ingested and analyzed, Traxxia's AI engine equips your questionnaire with deep strategic insights and traceability:

### 1. Multi-Version Answer Management
For every strategic question, the interface allows you to view and toggle between different states of your answer:
*   **AI Answer**: The draft answer extracted directly from your uploaded strategic documents.
*   **Edited/User Answer**: The active, refined response saved by the user. Editing any answer block automatically sets its status to **Edited**.
*   **Previous Answer**: A secure record of the historical response before the latest AI ingestion, allowing you to track changes.

### 2. Confidence Level Assessment
*   The AI calculates a strategic **Confidence Score** for each extracted answer. 
*   This score highlights the reliability and completeness of the extracted information relative to the question, making it easy to see which areas need review.

### 3. Citation & Traceability Tracking
*   **Source Citations**: Extracted answers are tagged with direct citations pointing to the specific uploaded document(s) used as evidence.
*   **Dynamic Cleanup**: If you remove an uploaded document from the library, the system automatically runs a cleanup sweep, updating or clearing any active citations related to that file.

### 4. Interactive Editing
*   You can edit, refine, or write completely new answers directly within the text area of any questionnaire block, allowing you to fine-tune AI suggestions with absolute control.
 

## Ingestion & Analysis Workflow

### Step 1 — Open the Ingestion Pane
1.  Navigate to the **Analysis** tab of your business profile.
2.  Click on the **Multiple File Upload** accordion card on the left panel to expand it.

### Step 2 — Upload Your Documents
1.  Click the file upload area or drag-and-drop your strategic documents (PDF, DOCX, etc.) directly into the zone.
2.  The sequential engine will validate the formats and begin uploading them. You will see progress indicators next to each file.

![Multiple Document Ingestion Placement](/academy-screenshots/questionnaire/ai-answer-support.png)
*The Multiple File Upload accordion pane inside the Brief section.*

### Step 3 — Trigger AI Analysis
1.  Once your documents show a **Success** status, click the **"Analyze Document"** button.
2.  The Traxxia AI engine will parse the text and structure of your documents to extract critical answers to the strategic questions.
3.  The system will automatically pre-populate the questionnaire answers, keeping you from having to type or copy-paste long paragraphs manually.
 

> [!TIP] You can remove any uploaded file at any time by clicking the trash can icon next to the file name. Removing a file will immediately clean up the file reference from your strategic workspace.

> [!IMPORTANT]  To ensure the AI extracts high-quality, relevant answers, make sure your strategic PDF or Word documents contain clear explanations of your customer base, business model, competitors, and strategic objectives.
