## Editing & Auto-save

Strategizing is an iterative process. As your business evolves, you can easily update your answers in the **Analysis** tab.

## 1. Editing Answers
To update an answer:
1.  **Click to Edit**: Click on any answer or the "Edit" button next to a question.
2.  **Modify Content**: Enter the new information in the text area.
3.  **Manual Save**: Click the "Save" button to apply changes immediately.

## 2. Auto-save Feature
Traxxia includes a smart **Auto-save** mechanism to protect your work:
- **Automatic Sync**: After 3 seconds of inactivity while typing, your changes are automatically saved to the database.
- **Success Indicators**: You will see a small "Auto-saved!" toast message and a status checkmark when the sync is complete.

## 3. Applying AI-Suggested Answers
The most efficient way to build your profile is to use the **Generate AI Answers** feature with the **Apply All** option.

- **Triggering Results**: Unlike manual edits, clicking **"Apply All AI Answers"** triggers a comprehensive background process.
- **Strategic API Call**: This action calls the **Strategic API**, which correlates your answers across different business dimensions.
- **Automatic Regeneration**: The system automatically regenerates all relevant SWOT, Porter's, and PESTEL insights to ensure they align with the newly applied data.

> [!NOTE]
> Manual edits and individual saves do not automatically regenerate all analysis records. Global regeneration is reserved for when you apply a full set of AI-suggested answers to maintain system efficiency and data consistency.
