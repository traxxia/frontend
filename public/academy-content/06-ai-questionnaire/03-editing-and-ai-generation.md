# Editing & AI Generation

Strategizing is an iterative process. As your business evolves, you can easily update your answers in the **Analysis** tab using manual edits, onboarding-based generation, or secure strategic document ingestion.

## 1. Editing Answers

To update an answer manually:
1.  **Click to Edit**: Click on any answer text area or the **"Edit"** button next to a question.
2.  **Modify Content**: Enter the new or updated information in the text area.
3.  **Manual Save**: Click the **"Save"** button to apply the changes immediately.


## 2. Auto-save Feature

Traxxia includes a smart **Auto-save** mechanism to protect your progress and prevent data loss:
-   **Automatic Sync**: After 3 seconds of inactivity while typing, your edits are automatically saved to the database.
-   **Success Indicators**: You will see a small "Auto-saved!" toast message and a status checkmark when the sync is complete.


## 3. Applying AI-Suggested Answers (Refine Answers with AI)

The most efficient way to build your profile is to use the **Refine Answers with AI** feature located in the **Refine AI Answers** accordion panel.

*   **Triggering Results**: Clicking **"Refine Answers with AI"** triggers a prompt: *"By doing this, this will overwrite all the existing answers and it will regenerate the insights and strategic analysis."* Upon confirmation, it initiates a comprehensive background process.
*   **Strategic API Call**: This action calls the **Strategic API**, which correlates your onboarding data across different business dimensions to draft responses.
*   **Automatic Regeneration**: The system automatically regenerates all relevant SWOT, Porter's, and PESTEL insights to ensure they align with the newly applied data.

> [!NOTE]  Manual edits and individual saves do not automatically regenerate all analysis records. Global regeneration is reserved for when you apply a full set of AI-suggested answers via **"Refine Answers with AI"** to maintain system efficiency and data consistency.

## 4. Ingesting Strategic Documents (AI Extraction)

For organizations with pre-existing documentation, you can bypass manual editing by letting the AI extract questionnaire answers from your business files:

*   **Document Upload**: Expand the **Multiple File Upload** accordion inside the Analysis tab and upload your strategic files (PDF, DOCX).
*   **Trigger Ingestion**: Click **"Analyze Document"**. The AI parser reads your documents, extracts relevant strategy details, and maps them to the appropriate strategic questions.
*   **Refinement**: Once the extraction is complete, review the pre-populated responses in the editor, make any necessary manual adjustments, and save.

> [!TIP]  Combining **Document Upload** with manual adjustments gives you the perfect balance of automated speed and strategic precision.
