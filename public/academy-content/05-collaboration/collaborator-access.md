# Collaborator Access

Understanding how to manage access for your collaborators is key to a secure and efficient collaborative environment.

## 1. Collaborator Dashboard.
When a collaborator assign to a business it will be under collaborating business Accordion in their dashboard.

![Collaborator Dashboard](/academy-screenshots/collaboration/collaborator-dashboard.png)
*Screenshot of Collaborator Dashboard with business*

Collaborator will have the same interface in business but the access can be differ by role

## 2. Benefits of Collaborators
- Able to view the business
- Able to answer the questions
- Able to access the insights
- Able to access the strategic framework
- Able to create projects and rank them

> [!NOTE] Only Company Admins can manage collaborator access across businesses and projects.
