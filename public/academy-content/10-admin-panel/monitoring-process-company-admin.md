# Monitoring Process for Company Admin

As a Company Admin (Org Admin), you have access to tools that help you monitor the progress of your organization's strategic analysis and project implementation.

## 1. Overview of Admin Panel Tabs
Depending on your role, you will see a subset of these specialized tabs:

-   **Company**: View of your own organization details like Industry, Size, Status, Exired Date etc...
-   **Businesses**: Manage individual workspaces (Business Entities). Monitor current strategic progress across your Organization.
-   **User Management**: The primary hub for viewing users, and filter by roles, Add and assign users to the businesses.
-   **Access Management**: Manage the users who have the access over the projects.
-   **User History**: A chronological record of strategic contributionsâ€”who completed what phase and when.
-   **Audit Trail**: A secure log targeting Organization-wide changes, logins, and sensitive administrative settings for compliance.
-   **Subscription**: A Hub where you can manage your subscription and payment history.

## 2. Key Metrics
-   Business created logs
-   Questionnaire completion logs
-   Subscription and payment history
-   Audit trail logs

> [!TIP] As a Company Admin, you can also access the **Audit Trail** to monitor user activity and ensure compliance with organizational policies.
