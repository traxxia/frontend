# The S.T.R.A.T.E.G.I.C Framework

The **S.T.R.A.T.E.G.I.C** framework is your roadmap for turning analysis into execution. It breaks down your business strategy into 9 actionable pillars across 3 core blocks.

## 1. Strategy Block (S.T.R): Direction & Positioning
- **S – Strategy**: Where to compete and how to differentiate.
- **T – Tactics**: Mapping high-level goals to concrete initiatives.
- **R – Resources**: Allocating capital, talent, and technology.

## 2. Execution Block (A.T.E): Implementation & Monitoring
- **A – Analysis**: Ensuring you measure what matters with a data strategy.
- **T – Technology**: Building the digital backbone and infrastructure.
- **E – Execution**: Managing the roadmap and monitoring KPIs.

## 3. Sustainability Block (G.I.C): Long-term Reinforcement
- **G – Governance**: Decision rights and accountability matrices.
- **I – Innovation**: Managing the future pipeline and portfolio mix.
- **C – Culture**: Aligning the human side of transformation.
