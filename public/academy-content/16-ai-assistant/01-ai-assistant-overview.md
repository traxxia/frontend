## How to Access the AI Assistant

You can access the AI Assistant from anywhere in the application (except for the login and register pages).

1.  Look for the **AI Assistant icon** in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
2.  Click the icon to open the chat panel.
3.  Type your question or select a suggestion to get started.

> [!TIP]
> You can also minimize the chat window to keep it accessible while you work on other tasks.

## Features

### 1. Interactive Chat
Engage in a natural conversation with the AI. Ask questions like:
- "How do I create a new project?"
- "What does my SWOT analysis say about my weaknesses?"
- "Help me prioritize my current projects."

![Interactive Chat Interface](/academy-screenshots/ai-assistant/chat-interface.png)

### 2. Contextual Suggestions
The AI Assistant proactively offers suggestions based on the page you are viewing. For example, if you are on the **Financial Analysis** page, it might suggest:
- "Analyze my latest income statement"
- "Explain the trends in my revenue"

## Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: The more details you provide, the better the AI can assist you.
- **Use Context**: Reference specific projects or documents in your questions.
- **Explore**: Don't be afraid to ask open-ended questions to discover new insights about your business.
