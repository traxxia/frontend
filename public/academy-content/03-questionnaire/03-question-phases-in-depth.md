# Question Phases In-Depth

Traxxia's AI questionnaire uses a **phased approach** to strategically gather information. Understanding these phases helps you plan your time and know what to expect at each stage.

---

## Overview of the Three Phases

| Phase | Questions | Unlock Condition | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|------------------|---------|
| **Initial** | ~10-15 | Available immediately | Foundation & basics |
| **Essential** | ~30-40 | Complete Initial phase | Core strategic information |
| **Advanced** | ~20-30 | Complete Essential + financial decision | Deep insights & optimization |

**Total Questions**: Approximately 60-85 questions (varies by business complexity)

---

## Phase 1: Initial Questions

### Purpose
Establish basic understanding of your business concept, mission, and market position.

### Topics Covered:
- Business vision and mission
- Core value proposition  
- Products or services offered
- Basic target market definition
- Company structure basics

### Sample Questions:
- "What is your business's primary purpose or mission?"
- "What core products or services does your business offer?"
- "In one sentence, what problem does your business solve?"
- "Where is your business located?"

### Completion Time
**Estimate**: 15-30 minutes

### Why These Questions Matter:
These questions set the context for all subsequent analysis. They help the AI understand:
- What industry you're in
- What you sell/provide
- Who you serve
- Your basic business model

### Tips for This Phase:
âœ… Be concise but clear  
âœ… Focus on high-level overview  
âœ… Don't over-think - details come later  
âœ… Complete in one session if possible

## Financial Upload Decision Point

### When It Appears:
After completing **100% of Essential questions**, the AI prompts:

```
"Great! You've completed the Essential phase. Would you like to upload 
financial data for enhanced analysis, or skip and continue with the 
remaining questions?"
```

### Your Options:

#### Option A: Upload Financial Data âœ…

**Benefits**:
- Unlock financial analysis frameworks
- Get profitability, growth, and risk metrics
- More accurate strategic recommendations
- Enhanced competitive benchmarking

**What to Upload**:
- Excel files based on Traxxia templates
- Financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- Historical data (1-3 years recommended)

**File Requirements**:
- Format: Excel (.xlsx, .xls) only
- Must match Traxxia template structure
- AI validates template before accepting

**When to Choose This**:
- You have financial data ready
- Want comprehensive financial analysis
- Business has historical financials
- Need data-driven strategic insights

#### Option B: Skip Upload â­ï¸

**Impact**:
- Proceed directly to Advanced questions
- Financial analysis sections will be limited
- Strategic analysis still fully functional
- Can upload later if you change your mind

**When to Choose This**:
- Don't have financial data available
- Early-stage startup without financials
- Privacy/confidentiality concerns
- Want to complete questionnaire first

> [!TIP]
> Skipping doesn't lock you out permanently. You can always return and upload financial data later from the business page.

### How to Decide:

**Choose Upload If:**
- âœ… You have 1+ years of financial data
- âœ… Financial performance is key to your strategy
- âœ… Want robust financial analysis
- âœ… Data is ready and in accessible format

**Choose Skip If:**
- âœ… Pre-revenue or very early stage
- âœ… Financial data not currently available
- âœ… Want to finish questionnaire quickly
- âœ… Can come back later with data

Gather **deep strategic insights** for optimization, innovation, and long-term competitive positioning.

### Why Advanced Questions Matter:
While Essential questions feed into immediate strategic frameworks, Advanced questions:
- Inform long-term strategic recommendations
- Identify innovation opportunities

- Enable scenario planning

## Strategic Skipping

### When to Skip Questions

**Valid Reasons**:
- Question truly not applicable to your business
- Information not currently available
- Requires research you'll do later
- Sensitive/confidential data you can't share

**Poor Reasons**:
- Question seems hard
- Don't feel like answering
- Want to finish quickly
- Think it doesn't matter

### Impact of Skipping

**Skipping Initial Questions**:
- May create gaps in basic understanding
- Could affect relevance of later questions
- Minimal skip - high impact per question

**Skipping Essential Questions**:
- Directly reduces strategic analysis quality
- May produce generic frameworks
- Significant gap in specific areas (e.g., skip all competitive questions â†’ weak competitive analysis)

**Skipping Advanced Questions**:
- Limits long-term strategic recommendations
- Reduces innovation insights
- Least immediate impact on core frameworks

### Best Practice:
Aim for 90%+ completion rate, especially in Essential phase

## Completing Phases Efficiently

### Strategy 1: Block Time
Set aside dedicated blocks:
- Session 1 (30 min): Initial phase
- Session 2 (2 hours): Essential phase (first half)
- Session 3 (2 hours): Essential phase (second half) + financial decision
- Session 4 (2 hours): Advanced phase

### Strategy 2: Delegate
Assign questions by expertise:
- CEO: Vision, strategy, market questions
- CFO: Financial, cost structure questions
- COO: Operations, value chain questions
- Sales: Customer, competitive questions

### Strategy 3: Iterative
- Complete all phases at 70-80% first pass
- Return and fill gaps
- Update answers as you get better information

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I skip a phase?**  
A: No. Phases unlock sequentially. You must complete (or skip questions within) each phase to unlock the next.

**Q: Can I go back to a previous phase?**  
A: Yes. You can always scroll up and change answers to earlier questions.

**Q: What if I complete Essential but want to add more info later?**  
A: You can return anytime and update answers. May trigger re-validation by AI.

**Q: Do I have to upload financial data?**  
A: No. It's optional. Skipping still lets you complete Advanced questions.

**Q: How long until answers "expire"?**  
A: Never. Your answers are saved indefinitely unless you delete the business.
