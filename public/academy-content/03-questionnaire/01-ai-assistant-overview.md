## Question Phases

The questionnaire is organized into **three phases** that unlock progressively:

### Phase 1: Initial Questions
**Purpose**: Basic business understanding

**Topics**:
- Business vision and mission
- Core value proposition
- Target market basics

**Completion**: Required to unlock Essential phase

---

### Phase 2: Essential Questions â­
**Purpose**: Critical strategic information

**Topics**:
- Competitive landscape
- Market dynamics
- Business model details
- Customer analysis
- Value chain

**Completion**: Required before financial upload option appears

**Importance**: These questions directly feed into SWOT, PESTEL, and other strategic analyses.

> [!IMPORTANT]

**Purpose**: Deep strategic insights

**Topics**:

**Unlock Condition**: Complete Essential phase AND make financial upload decision (upload or skip)

## AI Follow-Up Questions

### Why Follow-Ups Happen

The AI requests more information when answers are:
- Too brief or vague
- Missing critical context
- Unclear or ambiguous
- Lacking specific examples

### Example Exchange:

**Initial Question**: "Describe your target market."

**Your Answer**: "Small businesses"

**AI Follow-Up**: "Could you provide more details about these small businesses? For example, what industry are they in, what size (revenue/employees), and what specific problems do they face?"

**Better Answer**: "Our target market is dental practices with 2-10 dentists, generating $500K-$2M in annual revenue. They struggle with patient scheduling inefficiencies, high no-show rates, and administrative overhead."

### How Auto-Save Works:
- Answers save as you type (debounced)

> [!TIP]

## Skipping Questions

### When to Skip:
- Question not applicable to your business
- Don't have information available yet
- Want to research before answering

### How to Skip:
1. Click the **"Skip"** button below the question
2. Confirmation appears
3. Question marked as skipped
4. Can return and answer later

**Impact of Skipping**:
- Progress still advances
- Strategic analysis may be less comprehensive
- Can un-skip by providing an answer later

## Best Practices

### 1. Be Thorough & Specific

**Weak Answer**: "We sell consulting services."

**Strong Answer**: "We provide AI strategy consulting to mid-market financial services companies (100-1000 employees), helping them identify AI use cases, develop implementation roadmaps, and select appropriate vendors. Average engagement: $50K over 3 months."

### 2. Provide Context

Include:
- Relevant numbers and metrics
- Timeframes and historical data
- Specific examples
- Comparative information

### 3. Answer Honestly

- Don't embellish or exaggerate
- Acknowledge uncertainties
- Provide best estimates when exact data unavailable
- The AI analysis is only as good as your input

### 4. Use the AI Follow-Ups

- Don't be frustrated by follow-up questions
- They help you think more deeply about strategy
- Better answers = better strategic analysis
- Think of it as a strategic consultant interviewing you

## Question Completion

### When is a Question "Complete"?

A question is marked complete when:
1. You submit an answer
2. AI validates it (no follow-ups needed OR follow-ups answered)
3. Answer is saved to database

**OR**

- You explicitly skip the question

---

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: AI Keeps Asking Follow-Ups

**Cause**: Answers are too brief or vague

**Solution**:
- Read the follow-up carefully
- Provide specific details requested
- Include examples and context
- Aim for 2-4 sentences minimum

---

### Issue: Don't Know the Answer

**Options**:
1. Skip the question for now
2. Provide best estimate with caveats
3. Delegate to team member who knows
4. Research and return later

---

### Issue: Progress Not Updating

**Solution**:
- Wait for auto-save to complete
- Refresh the page
- Check that answer was submitted (press Enter/Send)
- Verify internet connection

---

### Issue: Want to Change an Answer

**Solution**:
1. Scroll up in chat to find the question
2. Type a new answer in the input field
3. Submit the new answer
4. Previous answer is overwritten

> [!WARNING]
> Changing answers may require re-validation by the AI, potentially triggering new follow-up questions.
