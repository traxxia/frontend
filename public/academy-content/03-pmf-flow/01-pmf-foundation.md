# PMF Foundation & Onboarding

The Product-Market Fit (PMF) Flow is the primary portal for strategic value in Traxxia. It is designed as a **High-Velocity Onboarding** experience that transforms your business context into directional clarity in minutes.

---

## 1. The "One Product Objective"

Traxxia's success is measured by how quickly you gain clarity. We focus on:

*   **15-Minute Milestone**: reach clear strategic priorities and next actions in under 15 minutes.
*   **Recognition over Analysis**: immediately recognize your business reality in the AI-generated data.
*   **Weekly Habit**: a concise output that encourages weekly updates to your execution plan.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The goal is to establish a strong "Good Enough" baseline quickly, which you can then refine over time as your strategy evolves.

---

## 2. The Onboarding Journey

The onboarding process consists of 9 mandatory steps. 

### Step 1: Identity & Presence
Identify the business you are analyzing.
*   **Fields**: Company / Client Name (Required), Website (Optional).
![Step 1](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Geographic Baseline
Establishes the location context for your industry.
*   **Fields**: Country (Required), City (Optional).
![Step 2](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Industry Classification
Maps your business to specialized market trends.
*   **Field**: Primary Industry.
![Step 3](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Strategic Scope
Define the specific boundaries for your strategy. 
*   **Field**: Strategic Geographies (Up to 3).
![Step 4](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-4.png)

### Step 5: The Profit Engine (Core)
Define your current profit sources across different dimensions.
*   **Fields**: Customer Segments, Products/Services, Channels.
![Step 5](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Primary Objective
Set your "North Star" goal.
*   **Options**: Grow, Improve Profitability, Focus/Simplify, etc.
![Step 6](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Primary Constraint
Identify the main bottleneck holding you back.
*   **Options**: Too many initiatives, Weak differentiation, Pricing pressure, etc.
![Step 7](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-7.png)

### Step 8: Differentiation (The "How")
Select how you win against the competition.
*   **Restriction**: Maximum 2 selections to remain strategically focused.
![Step 8](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-8.png)

### Step 9: Usage Context
Specify the context for the final output.
*   **Options**: My Company vs. A Client.
![Step 9](/academy-screenshots/pmf/step-9.png)

---

## 3. Real-time Progress

As you move through the journey, the progress bar indicates how close you are to generating your initial strategy. Once finished, the system generates your **AHA Insights** and **Executive Summary** in parallel, ready for immediate review.
