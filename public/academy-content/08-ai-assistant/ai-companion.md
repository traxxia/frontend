# The AI Assistant

Your **AI Assistant** is more than just a chatbot—it is a continuous data companion that lives within both your business and project views to provide real-time intelligence.

## 1. Using the AI Assistant
The AI Assistant is accessible via the floating sparkle icon at the bottom right of your screen. When clicked, it opens a chat panel where you can interact with the AI to get guidance on your business strategy and project details.

### Contextual Intelligence
The AI Assistant is aware of where you are in the application:
-   **Questionnaire View**: Provides high-level business advice and helps you navigate the platform.
-   **Project Edit View**: Automatically receives the current **Project ID**, allowing it to answer specific questions about that initiative’s goals, risks, and descriptions.

## 2. Monitoring Usage
To ensure fair access for all users, AI usage is monitored based on "tokens" consumed during conversations.

### Usage Indicator
In the top header of the AI panel, you will see a circular progress bar. This indicator shows the percentage of your monthly AI token quota that has been used:
-   **Green**: Low usage.
-   **Yellow (70%+)**: Approaching the limit.
-   **Red (90%+)**: Very close to the limit.

> [!NOTE] 
> Hovering over the circle will show your exact token count (e.g., Used: 150,000 / 3,000,000).

### Limit Exceeded
If you reach your plan's token limit:
-   The input field will be disabled.
-   A message will appear: **"Limit Reached. Quota Resets At: [Date]"**.
-   The assistant will provide a final message explaining the situation.

## 3. Managing History
Your conversation history is unified across your session, meaning you can start a chat in one part of the app and continue it in another.

### Clear History Button
If you wish to reset the conversation, you can use the **Clear History** (trash icon) button in the header.
-   Clicking this will trigger a **confirmation modal** to prevent accidental deletion.
-   Clearing history permanently removes the current thread and starts a fresh conversation.

## 4. Dynamic Suggestions
Based on the field you are editing or the phase you are in, the AI will suggest relevant questions to help you progress faster, such as:
-   "Help me refine the project description."
-   "What risks should I consider for this initiative?"
-   "Suggest success metrics for this strategic bet."

> [!TIP] Use the AI as a "sparring partner" to challenge your strategic assumptions and help you write more professional business descriptions.
