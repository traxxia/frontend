# The AI Questionnaire

Once your business is set up, it's time to talk to the AI. The questionnaire is the heart of Traxxia, where raw data is converted into strategic intelligence.

## 1. Product-Market Fit (PMF) Flow
In Phase 3, Traxxia introduces a dedicated **PMF Flow**. This is a specialized workflow designed to help you validate your business positioning and ensure that your value proposition aligns with your target market's needs.

## 2. Navigating the Phases
The questionnaire is divided into three distinct phases:

### **Phase 1: Essential (Discovery)**
-   **Goal**: Define the core purpose and value proposition.
-   **Outcome**: Strategic themes and PMF validation.
-   **Note**: Available to both Essential and Advanced users.

### **Phase 2: Good (Depth)**
-   **Goal**: Deepen analysis with competitive positioning.
-   **Outcome**: SWOT analysis and detailed market dynamics.

### **Phase 3: Advanced (Alignment)**
-   **Goal**: Comprehensive strategic blueprint and risk assessment.
-   **Outcome**: Complete readiness for project kickstarting.

> [!TIP] The Essential plan offers **Unlimited Initiatives** within your single workspace, allowing you to iterate through as many strategic ideas as you need until you find the right path.
