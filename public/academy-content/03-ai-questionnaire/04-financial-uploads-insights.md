# Financial Uploads & Insights

For businesses with existing financial history, Traxxia allows you to bypass manual data entry by uploading financial documents directly.

## 1. Uploading Financial Data
At the top of the **Advanced** tab, you will find the **Financial Data** block.
- **Accepted Files**: Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV files.
- **Template Types**: Traxxia auto-detects if your file matches our **Simplified, Standard, or Detailed** report templates.

## 2. Unlocking Financial Insights
Uploading a valid financial document unlocks a specialized suite of performance reports:
- **Profitability Analysis**: Comparison of margins vs. industry standards.
- **Growth Tracker**: Trend analysis of revenue and net income.
- **Liquidity & Efficiency**: Working capital, inventory turnover, and cash flow cycles.
- **Investment Performance**: In-depth ROA, ROE, and ROIC analysis.
- **Leverage & Risk**: Financial stability assessment with traffic-light risk indicators.

## 3. Summary of Unlocked Insights
Depending on your phase and document status, the **Insights (6 C's)** tab organizes results into six critical dimensions:

1.  **Costs/Financial**: Performance and efficiency (unlocked by Financial Upload).
2.  **Context/Industry**: Market forces and PESTEL/SWOT dynamics.
3.  **Customer**: Behavior, loyalty (NPS), and purchase criteria.
4.  **Capabilities**: Internal strengths and maturity scores.
5.  **Competition**: Landscape and competitive advantage matrix.
6.  **Current Strategy**: Strategic core and adjacency analysis.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> To ensure the highest accuracy, use the provided templates for your financial uploads. You can download these templates directly from the Financial Templates popup.
