# First Login Experience

Congratulations on creating your Traxxia account! This guide walks you through your first login and helps you get oriented with the platform.

## What to Expect

Your first login experience varies slightly depending on your role:

- **Org Admin, User, Collaborator (with User role)**: Dashboard with option to create your first business
- **Super Admin**: Dashboard with view-only access to all businesses
- **Collaborator (without User role), Viewer**: Dashboard showing businesses you've been invited to

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Traxxia

1. **Open your browser** and go to your Traxxia URL
2. **Bookmark the page** for quick access in

 the future
3. **Click "Login"** or go directly to the login page

![Traxxia login page](/academy-screenshots/getting-started/login-page.png)

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Email Address**: Enter the email you registered with
2. **Password**: Type your secure password
3. **Show/Hide Password**: Click the eye icon to toggle password visibility
4. **Remember Device** (if available): Check to stay logged in

> [!TIP]
> If you saved your password in your browser's password manager, it will auto-fill for convenience.

---

## Step 3: Two-Factor Authentication (If Enabled)

If your organization has enabled 2FA:

1. Enter the verification code from your authenticator app
2. Or enter the code sent to your email/phone
3. Check "Trust this device" to skip 2FA on this device for 30 days

> [!NOTE]
> Two-factor authentication is currently planned for a future release.

---

## Step 4: Initial Dashboard Load

After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to your dashboard.

### What You'll See:

**For Org Admin/User:**
```
✓ Welcome message
✓ "Create New Business" button (prominent)
✓ Empty state or existing businesses
✓ Navigation menu with all features
✓ User menu in top-right corner
```

![Dashboard for new admin user](/academy-screenshots/getting-started/dashboard-orgadmin-first-login.png)

**For Super Admin:**
```
✓ Welcome message 
✓ View-only dashboard showing all organization businesses
✓ Admin and user management features
✓ NO "Create New Business" button
✓ User menu in top-right corner
```

**For Collaborators (with User role):**
```
✓ Welcome message
✓ "Create New Business" button
✓ List of businesses you're assigned to
✓ Access to assigned business features
```

**For Collaborators (without User role):**
```
✓ Welcome message
✓ List of businesses you're assigned to  
✓ Limited navigation (no business creation)
✓ Access to assigned business features
```

**For Viewers:**
```
✓ Read-only dashboard
✓ List of businesses you can view
✓ Notice about read-only access
✓ Limited action buttons
```

## Step 5: Explore the Interface

### Top Navigation Bar

The menubar at the top provides access to key features:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Traxxia Logo** | Click to return to dashboard |
| **User Menu** | Profile, settings, logout |

![Top navigation bar showing logo and user menu](/academy-screenshots/getting-started/dashboard-with-menu-open.png)

### User Menu Options

Click your profile icon (top-right) to access:

- **Dashboard**: Return to main dashboard
- **Traxxia Academy**: Access help documentation (this site!)
- **Super Admin Panel**: System administration (Super Admins only)
- **Admin**: Company management (Company Admins only)
- **Logout**: End your session

> [!NOTE]
> Profile update features are planned for a future release. For now, contact your organization administrator if you need to update your account information.

## Step 6: Set Your Preferences

Before diving in, configure your account:

### Language Preference

Traxxia supports multiple languages:

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The language selector is **only available on the login page**. Choose your preferred language (English or LATAM Spanish) before signing in.

**To change language after login:**
1. Log out of your account
2. Return to the login page  
3. Select your preferred language from the selector
4. Log back in

> [!TIP]
> Your language preference is saved to your browser session and will persist until you log out.

## Step 7: Understand Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is organized by business status:

### Business Organization

Businesses are divided into two accordion sections:

1. **Businesses Without Projects**
   - Businesses still in strategic analysis phase
   - Expand accordion to view all businesses in this category
   - Focus on completing questionnaires and reviewing analyses
   - Progress bars show AI questionnaire completion

2. **Businesses in Project Phase**
   - Businesses with created strategic projects
   - Expand accordion to view all businesses in this category
   - Focus on project prioritization and execution
   - Project count shown for each business

![Business cards showing different statuses](/academy-screenshots/getting-started/dashboard-business-list.png)

> [!TIP]
> Click the accordion headers to expand/collapse each section. This keeps your dashboard organized as your business portfolio grows.

### Business Card Information

Each business card displays:
- **Business name and description**
- **Progress indicator**: Shows AI questionnaire completion percentage
- **Status badges**: Current phase (Prioritizing, Prioritized, etc.)
- **Quick action buttons**: Open, edit, or delete (based on permissions)

### Quick Actions

Depending on your role:
- **Create New Business**: Start a new strategic initiative
- **View Projects**: Access strategic project management
- **Access Analysis**: Review strategic frameworks

## Common First-Login Tasks

### Task 1: Create Your First Business

If you have business creation privileges (Org Admin, User, or Collaborator with User role):

1. Click **"Create New Business"** on the dashboard
2. Fill in required information:
   - Business name
   - Business purpose
   - Description
   - City/Country
3. Click **"Create Business"**
4. You'll be directed to the business setup page

> [!TIP]
> See our guide on [Creating Your First Business](../02-businesses/01-creating-your-first-business.md) for detailed instructions.

> [!NOTE]
> Super Admins do not have the "Create New Business" button as they have view-only access to business data.

### Task 2: Review Invited Businesses (Collaborators)

If you're a Collaborator:

1. Review the businesses on your dashboard
2. Click a business card to open it
3. Familiarize yourself with the business details
4. Check for any assigned tasks or questions

### Task 3: Explore Available Features

Take 5-10 minutes to:

1. Click through the navigation menu
2. Open Traxxia Academy (this documentation!)
3. Check what features are available for your role
4. Familiarize yourself with the interface layout

## Troubleshooting First Login Issues

### Issue: "Invalid Credentials"

**Solution:**
- Double-check your email address (case-sensitive)
- Verify your password is correct
- Click "Show Password" to see what you're typing
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed

### Issue: "Account Not Verified"

**Solution:**
- Check your email inbox for verification link
- Look in spam/junk folder
- Click the verification link
- Request a new verification email if link expired

### Issue: Can't See Any Businesses

**Possible Reasons:**
- You haven't been invited to any businesses yet
- You haven't created any businesses yet (requires admin role)
- Contact your administrator

**Solution:**
- If you're an admin, create your first business
- If you're a collaborator, wait for an invitation
- Confirm with your Company Admin that you have access

### Issue: Missing Features or Buttons

**Possible Reasons:**
- Role-based access control (your role doesn't permit access)
- Feature not yet available in your organization

**Solution:**
- Check [Understanding User Roles](./03-understanding-user-roles.md) to see what your role can access
- Contact your administrator if you need different permissions

## Tips for Success

### 1. Don't Rush
Take time to explore the interface. Traxxia is powerful but intuitive once you understand the layout.

### 2. Use Traxxia Academy
Bookmark this documentation! We've created comprehensive guides for every feature.

### 3. Start Small
If you're creating a business, start with one straightforward project to learn the system.

### 4. Ask Questions
Use the AI chat assistant (when available) for quick help, or contact your administrator.

### 5. Leverage Collaboration
Invite team members early in the process to get diverse perspectives on strategic questions.

## Security Reminders

> [!WARNING]
> **Always log out** when using shared or public computers. Your session token provides access to sensitive business data.

### Best Practices:
- Never share your password
- Keep your session active only when actively using Traxxia
- Report any suspicious activity to your administrator
- Don't let others use your account to perform actions
