# Updating Your Profile

Keep your Traxxia profile current to ensure accurate notifications, proper attribution, and smooth collaboration with your team.

## Accessing Your Profile

### Quick Access
1. Click on your **profile icon** or **name** in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Profile"** or **"Account Settings"** from the dropdown
3. Your profile page will open

![Profile menu access from top navigation](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

> [!TIP]
> You can also access your profile from the main menu under "Settings" → "Profile"

## Profile Information Fields

### Basic Information

| Field | Description | Editable | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Full Name** | Your first and last name | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| **Email Address** | Primary contact email | ⚠️ Contact support | ✅ Yes |
| **Job Title** | Your role in the organization | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| **Phone Number** | Contact phone | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| **Department** | Your team or department | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |

![Profile information form](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> To change your email address, please contact support at `support@traxxia.com`. Email changes require verification for security.

### Profile Photo

Add a professional photo to help teammates identify you:

1. Click **"Upload Photo"** or **"Change Photo"**
2. Select an image from your computer
3. Crop/adjust if needed
4. Click **"Save"**

**Photo Requirements**:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **Max Size**: 2MB
- **Recommended**: Square aspect ratio (e.g., 400x400px)
- **Best Practice**: Use a clear, professional headshot

![Profile photo upload interface](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

### Timezone Settings

Ensure accurate timestamps and meeting scheduling:

1. Navigate to **"Timezone"** in profile settings
2. Select your timezone from the dropdown
3. System will auto-detect based on location (verify accuracy)
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

> [!NOTE]
> Timezone affects when you see timestamps and scheduled reports.

## Security & Privacy

### Password Management

Update your password regularly for security:

1. Go to **"Security"** tab in profile settings
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter new password (8+ characters recommended)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

![Password change interface](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

**Password Best Practices**:
- Use at least 8 characters
- Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid common words or personal information
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites
- Change password every 90 days

## Updating Profile - Step by Step

### Standard Profile Update

**Step 1**: Access profile
- Click your name in top-right
- Select "Profile"

**Step 2**: Make changes
- Update desired fields
- Upload new photo (optional)
- Adjust notification preferences
- Modify timezone if needed

**Step 3**: Save changes
- Click **"Save Changes"** or **"Update Profile"**
- Confirm success message appears
- Changes take effect immediately

![Profile update confirmation message](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

> [!TIP]
> Remember to save! Changes are not auto-saved—you must click "Save Changes" to apply updates.

## Troubleshooting

### Changes Not Saving

**Problem**: Profile updates don't persist after save

**Solutions**:
1. Check for error messages
2. Ensure all required fields are filled
3. Verify internet connection
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Log out and back in
6. Contact support if issue persists

### Photo Upload Fails

**Problem**: Profile photo won't upload

**Possible Issues**:
- File too large (reduce to under 2MB)
- Unsupported format (use JPG or PNG)
- Slow internet connection (wait and retry)
- Browser issue (try different browser)

### Email Change Not Working

**Problem**: Cannot update email address

**Solution**: Email changes require support verification
- Email `support@traxxia.com`
- Provide current and desired new email
- Verify ownership of both addresses
- Support will process after verification
