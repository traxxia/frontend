# What is Traxxia?

Traxxia is an AI-powered strategic business planning platform designed to help you analyze, strategize, and manage your business initiatives with confidence. Whether you're a startup founder, business consultant, or corporate strategist, Traxxia provides the tools you need to make data-driven decisions.

## Who Uses Traxxia?

Traxxia is designed for various user roles within your organization:

| Role | What They Can Do |
|------|------------------|
| **Org Admin** | Create businesses, manage users, access all features, control organization settings |
| **User** | Create and manage their own businesses, collaborate on shared businesses |
| **Collaborator** | Contribute to specific businesses, work on assigned projects |
| **Viewer** | View business information and analysis results (read-only) |

> [!TIP]
> Not sure which role you have? Check your profile in the user menu (top-right corner).

## How Traxxia Works

1. **Create a Business Profile** - Start by setting up your business or client project
2. **Answer AI Questions** - Our intelligent questionnaire gathers key information
3. **Generate Insights** - Traxxia analyzes your responses using proven frameworks
4. **Plan Projects** - Convert insights into actionable strategic initiatives
5. **Track Progress** - Monitor implementation and adjust as needed
