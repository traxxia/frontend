# Consensus Analysis

Once all collaborators have submitted their rankings, the Admin uses the **Ranking View** to make the final strategic decision.

## 1. AI Rank vs. Team Consensus
This view provides a side-by-side comparison of:
- **AI Recommendation**: The order suggested by Traxxia's models based on project data.
- **Team Consensus**: The aggregated average of all collaborator rankings.
- **Consensus Indicator**: A visual badge identifying alignment or divergence between the AI and human views.

![Consensus Matrix](/academy-screenshots/projects/ai-consensus1.png)

## 2. The "Team View" Tab
To dive deeper into team perspectives, the Admin can switch to the **Team View** tab.
- This tab shows exactly how each individual collaborator ranked every project.
- Red/Green indicators highlight where collaborators agreed or disagreed significantly with the collective view.
