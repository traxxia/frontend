# Projects Overview & States

After the kickstart process, all created initiatives move into the **Projects Section**. This area is the central hub for your strategic bets and is visible to all **Admins** and **Collaborators** assigned to the business.

## 1. Initial Project States
When a project first appears in the list, it is assigned two key initial states:
- **Lifecycle Status: Draft**: The project is in its definition phase. It cannot be launched until it has been ranked and refined.
- **Learning State: Testing**: This indicates that the strategic assumptions behind the project are currently under evaluation using the Traxxia methodology.

![Projects Dashboard](/academy-screenshots/projects/projects-overview.png)
*All projects start as Drafts, visible to the entire team.*

## 2. Viewing Your Initiatives
The Projects tab provides two ways to browse your work:
- **Card View**: A visual dashboard showing high-level metadata such as **Impact**, **Risk**, and **Effort**, along with the current ranking badge.
- **List View**: A tabular layout for quick scanning of project owners and timestamps.

## 3. Status Filters
At the top of the page, you can filter projects by their lifecycle stage:
- **All**: Every project in the business.
- **Draft**: Projects being refined or ranked.
- **Active**: Projects currently in execution.
- **At Risk / Paused / Killed / Scaled**: Historical or monitored states.

Each filter displays a **count badge**, showing you at a glance the distribution of your portfolio.
