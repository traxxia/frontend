# Finalize Prioritization Button

The "Finalize Prioritization" action marks the transition from active planning to a closed state, ready for launch.

## 1. Access Control

This critical action is restricted to:
*   **Organization Admins**
*   **Project Owners**

## 2. Before Finalization

Before clicking the Finalize button, ensure:

![Finalize Prioritization button](/academy-screenshots/projects/finalize-prioritization.png)


*   **All Votes Cast:** All stakeholders have completed their questionnaires and ranking inputs.
*   **Analysis Complete:** SWOT and PESTEL analyses have been reviewed.
*   **Consensus Reached:** There is general agreement on the project priorities.

## 3. After Finalization

Once finalized:
*   **Voting Closes:** No further votes or ranking changes can be made.
*   **Content Locked:** Questionnaires and analysis documents become read-only.
*   **Launch Ready:** The project status updates to "Ready for Launch", enabling the Launch button.
