Traxxia automatically tracks your business's progress through different stages. Understanding these statuses helps you know where you are in the strategic analysis journey.

## Detailed Status Descriptions

### 1. Prioritizing

**What it means**: You're actively working through the AI questionnaire.

**Characteristics**:
- Questionnaire is incomplete
- Some phases may be locked
- Analyses not yet generated
- Progress indicator shows completion percentage

**What you can do**:
- Answer questionnaire questions
- Save progress and return later
- Edit previous answers
- View partial progress

![Business card showing Prioritizing status with progress bar](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

**When it changes**:
- Automatically moves to **Prioritized** when you complete all required questionnaire phases

> [!TIP]
> The progress bar shows how close you are to completion. Aim to complete all three phases for full analysis!

### 2. Prioritized

**What it means**: Questionnaire is complete; strategic analyses have been generated.

**Characteristics**:
- All questionnaire phases completed
- Strategic analyses available (SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, etc.)
- Financial analyses ready (if documents uploaded)
- Projects can be created
- Ready for decision-making

![Business card showing Prioritized status](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

**What you can do**:
- Review strategic analyses
- Export reports to PDF
- Create strategic projects
- Share with collaborators
- Re-enter questionnaire to refine

**When it changes**:
- Moves to **Reprioritizing** if you re-enter the questionnaire
- Manually change to **Launched** when you implement the business

> [!IMPORTANT]
> "Prioritized" doesn't mean complete‚Äîit means you have enough information to make strategic decisions. You can always refine further!

### 3. Reprioritizing

**What it means**: You're updating or refining your questionnaire responses.

**Characteristics**:
- Previously completed questionnaire
- Now being edited or updated
- Previous analyses still available
- New analyses will regenerate after updates

![Business card showing Reprioritizing status](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

**Why reprioritize?**
- New market information emerged
- Business assumptions changed
- Initial answers were incomplete
- Competitive landscape shifted
- Financial situation updated

**What you can do**:
- Edit questionnaire answers
- Add missing information
- Correct errors or assumptions
- Expand on previous responses

**When it changes**:
- Returns to **Prioritized** when you complete the questionnaire updates
- Analyses automatically regenerate with new information

> [!TIP]
> Reprioritizing is healthy! Strategic analysis should evolve as you learn more about your business and market.

### 4. Launched

**What it means**: The business idea has been implemented in the real world.

**Characteristics**:
- Manually set by user
- Indicates active/operational business
- Analyses remain available for reference
- Historical snapshot preserved

![Business card showing Launched status](ALT_TEXT_PLACEHOLDER)

**How to set to Launched**:
1. Open business card menu (‚ãÆ)
2. Select **"Mark as Launched"**
3. Confirm action
4. Status updates immediately

**What you can do**:
- Keep business for reference
- Compare planned vs. actual performance
- Archive if no longer relevant
- Continue using analyses for optimization

> [!NOTE]
> Once marked as "Launched," you can still update the questionnaire and analyses‚Äîit's not a permanent lock!

## Status Indicators

### Dashboard View

On your dashboard, status is shown multiple ways:

**Visual Indicators**:
- **Status badge** on business card (color-coded)
- **Progress bar** (for Prioritizing status)
- **Icon** representing current stage
- **Last updated** timestamp

### Filtering by Status

Use filters to find businesses by status:

1. Click **"Filter"** on dashboard
2. Select one or more statuses
3. Click **"Apply"**
4. View filtered results

**Common filter uses**:
- View all **Prioritizing** businesses to finish pending work
- Find **Prioritized** businesses ready for decision
- Track **Launched** businesses separately

## Status Best Practices

### 1. Don't Rush Through Prioritizing

- Take time to answer questions thoughtfully
- Use all three questionnaire phases
- Provide detailed, specific responses
- Quality over speed!

### 2. Leverage Reprioritizing

- Update as you learn more
- Don't treat initial analysis as final
- Revisit quarterly or when major changes occur
- Refine based on market feedback

### 3. Use Launched Strategically

- Reserve for actual implementations
- Don't mark as launched prematurely
- Use it to track portfolio performance
- Archive launched businesses that are no longer active

### 4. Clean Up Regularly

- Review **Prioritizing** businesses monthly
- Delete abandoned business ideas
- Archive launched businesses after closure
- Keep active portfolio focused

## Troubleshooting Status Issues

### Stuck in Prioritizing

**Problem**: Can't get to Prioritized status

**Causes**:
- Incomplete questionnaire phases
- Required questions unanswered
- Validation errors in responses

**Solutions**:
1. Check progress bar percentage
2. Review all three phases
3. Look for red error indicators
4. Answer all required questions

[Learn about questionnaire completion](../03-questionnaire/04-tracking-your-progress.md)

### Status Not Updating

**Problem**: Completed questionnaire but still shows Prioritizing

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Check for error messages
- Verify all phases are complete
- Log out and back in
- Contact support if persists

### Can't Mark as Launched

**Problem**: "Mark as Launched" option is grayed out

**Causes**:
- You're not the business owner
- You're a Viewer (read-only)
- Business must be in Prioritized status first

**Solutions**:
- Complete questionnaire if in Prioritizing
- Contact business owner
- Ask Org Admin for ownership transfer
